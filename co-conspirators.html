04—Operations

On finding the inefficiencies and replacing them with something that scales.

Chase stands out at Jet for his ability to quickly find the inefficiencies in our internal processes and replace them with effective, scalable solutions that have helped Jet to become nimbler and more productive. He has proven very adept at breaking convoluted problems into discrete logical parts and finds the resources he needs to solve them.

Prior to Chase, Jet had no coherent system in place for running a product promotion end to end. Spanning supply chain, operations, marketing and sales, this is a complicated process with many moving parts and the need for cooperation from many different stakeholders. After running his first promotion event, Chase saw the need for a more structured process and took it upon himself to lead the effort to build a better solution.

His effort led to a much more scalable process that brought uniformity to how we run and track product promotions and provides transparency across the groups. Chase has proven very capable and intellectually curious, but importantly also very friendly and humble. He has been a pleasure to work with and will be a great asset to any firm.
