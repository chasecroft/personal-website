01 · TRAIL SIGNS

Do these sound familiar?

The companies are different. The knots are usually the same.

// STRATEGY
"We don't know what to say no to."
  • Look at your research, or run new, and tell you who the target market actually is
  • Create a process for turning work down with an org-wide read on the goals
  • Honest read on where competitors are already ahead
// ANALYTICS
"No one knows what data to look at."
  • Bring standard nomenclature to your data
  • North-star metric and P0 / P1 / P2 to drive decisions
  • Audit and retire data that doesn't make sense anymore
// RESEARCH
"I can't trust my research."
  • Set up microsurveys at the right moments in-app
  • Structured, moderated research with real hypotheses
  • Stop the leading questions; use software to synthesize
// PRODUCT
"Design is in discovery too long."
  • Build a backlog of ideas and check in regularly on the portfolio
  • Spin up a healthy PRD / design review cadence
  • Give direction on how to run sprints that actually ship
// RELIABILITY
"I don't know what tech debt to solve."
  • Create SLA guidelines for engineering
  • Weekly check-ins on reliability measures
  • Bring engineering into roadmapping; agree on when to pay debt
// OPERATIONS
"I have no visibility into team efficacy."
  • Set up your Linear space for ultimate transparency
  • Playbooks and tracking for customer-support efficacy
  • Identify internal tools to enable the team, and speed them up with AI
